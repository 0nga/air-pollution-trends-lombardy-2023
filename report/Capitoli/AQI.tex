\noindent
Looking at the table containing the air quality indices for the different provinces it is possible to make several observations:
\begin{itemize}
	\item May was the best 2023's month 
	\item February was the worst 2023's month
	\item in the months with high temperatures (from April to September) the index has lower values -> the air quality is higher
	\item in the months with low temperatures (from October to March) the index has higher values -> the air quality is lower
	\item the zone with the lowest AQI total is Lecco (LC)
	\item the  zone with the highest AQI total is Cremona (CR)
\end{itemize}
